        History of Phonak

Phonak was founded in Zurich, Switzerland in 1947 and has expanded globally with 123 international subsidiaries and distributors. Phonak is known for introducing innovative hearing solutions, including water-resistant hearing aids, models for managing tinnitus, and devices for single-sided hearing loss.

The company has been a pioneer in introducing new technologies such as the Spice Generation chip set and the myPhonak app for remote adjustments and support.

Phonak tinnitus solution

Tinnitus is often described as hearing sounds that aren't there, affecting about 10 to 15 percent of people.Tinnitus varies from a minor annoyance to a major disruptor of daily life.By causing sleep disturbances, anxiety, and concentration issues.

Fortunately, sound therapy, which can include various sounds like nature noises or music, paired with counseling, has proven effective in managing tinnitus.

Purchasing Phonak hearing aids

Phonak hearing aids are prescription aids, meaning that to acquire them, you must consult with a healthcare professional, such as an Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT) specialist or an audiologist. During the consultation, the healthcare professional will assess your hearing needs and recommend the most suitable type of hearing aids for you. This process involves discussing various factors, including the severity and type of hearing loss, your lifestyle, preferences, and budget.
