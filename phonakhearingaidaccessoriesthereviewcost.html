The Phonak RemoteControl offers a simple and intuitive way to manage your Phonak hearing aids without the need for a smartphone or app. Featuring a user-friendly design and tactile buttons, it allows for discreet adjustments on the go.

h3: Key Features:

  • Simple and Discreet Control: Easily adjust volume, check battery level, and view program status without fumbling or pulling out your phone.
  • Tactile Buttons: Clearly marked buttons for volume and program changes ensure easy navigation even with limited visibility.
  • User-friendly Design: Intuitive interface makes setup and use straightforward, even for first-time users.
  • Long Battery Life: Enjoy extended use with a long-lasting battery that's easy to replace.
  • TV Connector Activation: Activate your Phonak TV Connector directly from the remote control for enhanced TV listening experiences.
  • Compatible Hearing Aids: Works seamlessly with Phonak Lumity, Paradise, and Marvel hearing aids equipped with Bluetooth connectivity.
